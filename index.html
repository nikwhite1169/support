FAQ

Q: How do I use MenuWise to find the best menu items for my health goals at nearby restaurants?

A: Follow these simple steps:

  1. Open MenuWise and choose a health goal from the provided list.
  2. Select a nearby restaurant from the list that appears.
  3. MenuWise will suggest the best menu item based on your selected health goal.

Q: How does MenuWise determine the best menu items for my selected health goal?

A: MenuWise uses AI to analyze available nutritional information found on the restaurant's website or estimates it based on the description of the menu items if such information is not available. The AI then provides personalized recommendations that match your health goals.
